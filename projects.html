Our Research & Impact

Fair AI · Public Health · Open Science · Equity in Deployment

🔬 Flagship Toolkits (Developed at Vector Institute)

HumaniBench
A human-centered benchmark to evaluate fairness, empathy, multilingual performance, and robustness in large multimodal models.
FairSense-AI
A fairness and sustainability audit toolkit designed to evaluate LLMs and VLMs in terms of societal risk, regulatory alignment, and energy efficiency.
Unbias
A collaborative contribution to an explainability framework for bias identification in model predictions and pipelines.
