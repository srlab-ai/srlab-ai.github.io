🔬 Tools and Benchmarks (All Developed at Vector Institute)

HumaniBench
A human-centric benchmark for evaluating fairness, empathy, multilingual equity, and robustness in multimodal AI systems. Used to assess LLMs and VLMs in sensitive, real-world scenarios.
FairSense-AI
An open-source audit toolkit providing fairness metrics, sustainability analysis, and explainability overlays for evaluating responsible AI systems.
Unbias
A framework for explainable bias analysis in machine learning. We contributed to audit design and documentation, as part of the Bias and Explainability team at Vector Institute.

🏥 AI for Public Health Equity

Collaborators: Public Health Ontario · University of Toronto · Vector Institute
Our lab co-led research into equitable pandemic response, fair clinical triage systems, and representation-aware modeling in healthcare AI. Outcomes support risk-informed decision-making for vulnerable populations.
