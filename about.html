About Our Lab

Mission-driven. Human-centered. Technically excellent.

Who We Are

The Scientific Research Lab is a collaborative AI research group focused on building responsible, transparent, and high-impact AI systems. Our work spans across safety, health equity, ethical design, and multimodal AI development.

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI systems are equitable, interpretable, and safe. We support researchers, policy makers, and organizations in aligning AI development with human values.

What We Do

  • Conduct cutting-edge research in AI safety, robustness, and fairness.
  • Apply AI to solve real-world problems in public health and education.
  • Offer hands-on training and open courses on responsible AI practices.
  • Collaborate with academic, industry, and public sector stakeholders.
